FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with me

Who do you work with?

I work with both new and established authors across all genres—fiction, non-fiction, self-published, and traditionally published. Whether you're a debut author preparing to launch your first book or a seasoned writer looking to increase sales, I can help you achieve your goals.

How does the process work?

Simply reach out via email or the contact form. We'll discuss your book, goals, and I'll create a customized marketing strategy tailored to your needs. From there, we'll work together to implement the plan and track results.

How long does it take to see results?

Most authors see initial results within 2-4 weeks, with continued growth over the following months. Book marketing is a marathon, not a sprint! Sustainable success takes time, consistency, and the right strategies.

Do I need to have my book published already?

Not necessarily! I work with authors in the pre-launch phase as well as those with published books. Starting marketing before your book launches can lead to better results and more momentum on release day.

Do you work internationally?

Yes! I work with authors worldwide. All services are delivered remotely via email and online tools. As long as your book is available on major platforms like Amazon, I can help you reach readers globally.
